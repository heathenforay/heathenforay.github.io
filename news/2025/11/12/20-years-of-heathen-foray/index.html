20 Years of Heathen Foray!

Can you believe it? Today, we are thrilled to announce that we are celebrating our 20th Anniversary!

Two decades of pounding drums, searing riffs, epic lyrics, and unforgettable moments. From the bottom of our hearts, we want to send the biggest, loudest “THANK YOU” to you: Our fans.

Your unwavering support, your energy at the shows, your streams, and your messages have kept the fire burning for all these years.

In the coming weeks, we will share pics from the early days, old demo songs and announce a couple of new things as well!

Cheers to the past, and here’s to many more years of forging metal together!

PS: For those of you worrying why there are only 4 of us in the picture: Puma got sick the day we took the picture. He is well now and also joining the celebrations.

About the band

Heathen Foray

We are a Melodic Pagan Death Metal band from Austria. We are known for catchy melodies, skull splitting riffs and have played with bands like Ensiferum, Equilibirum, Eluveitie and Korpiklaani and toured Europe four times. We just released our 6th studio album Oathbreaker.
