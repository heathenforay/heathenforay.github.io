Biography

We are a Pagan Metal band from Austria. We are known for catchy melodies, skull splitting riffs and have played with bands like Ensiferum, Equilibirum, Eluveitie and Korpiklaani and toured Europe four times.

“One of the best bands Austria has to offer. Heavy as fuck and they still manage to write these awesome melodies. We need more of this band!”
